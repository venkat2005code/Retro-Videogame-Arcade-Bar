OUR STORY

The Golden Age, Resurrected

It started with a dusty basement, a few quarters, and a broken CRT monitor. We realized that the feeling of standing shoulder-to-shoulder with friends, illuminated only by the glow of a phosphor screen, was something the modern world was missing.

Arcade Bar was founded to resurrect that Golden Age. We've spent years hunting down, restoring, and maintaining authentic, original cabinets from the 80s and 90s.

Paired with an expertly curated selection of local craft beers and themed cocktails, we've created the ultimate space for both casual players and hardcore high-score chasers alike.
