FAQ

Are you 21+ only?
Yes, we strictly enforce a 21+ age limit after 8:00 PM. Before 8:00 PM on weekends, accompanied minors are welcome.
Do you serve food?
We offer a menu of shareable snacks and bites. Check our Services page for the full menu.
How much do games cost?
Most vintage cabinets are 2 tokens (50 cents). Pinball machines are 4 tokens ($1.00). Tuesdays are free play with a drink purchase.
